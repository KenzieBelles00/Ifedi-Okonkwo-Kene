Pancakes

What are pancakes? It's a famous snack eaten around the world. It is very tasty and easy to make as it takes very little time and has very few ingredients but if made well, makes you go for more all the time.

Ingredients

Not all recipes are the same. There are many ways to make pancakes but here is my list of ingredients for my own 5 star recipe.

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Syrup (Optional)
  • Vegetable oil

That's all the ingredients you would need for this recipe. Now, to actually make it...

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients: In a bowl, sift together the flour and the sugar. This ensures the batter is light and lump-free.
  2. Combine wet ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk the egg with the milk.
  3. Make the batter: Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently mix until just combined. The batter should be slightly lumpy. Do not overmix because it can make pancakes tough.
  4. Heat the pan: Preheat a non-stick frying pan over medium heat. Lightly grease with your veg oil.
  5. Cook the pancakes: Pour a small spoon of batter onto the pan for each pancake. Cook for about 2–3 minutes. Flip carefully and cook for another 1–2 minutes until golden brown on both sides.
  6. Serve: Stack the pancakes on a plate and serve warm. Popular toppings include maple syrup or a sprinkle of powdered sugar. I usually just add nothing.

And just like that, you've made yourself some delicious pancakes. Serve with any drink of your choice on the side and enjoy your meal!
